# **Risk Register Entry: \[TITLE OF RISK\]**

## **1\. Risk Definition**

| Risk ID | Title | Module(s) Impacted | Date Logged |
| :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- |

**Description:** Define the risk clearly. State the potential cause, the specific event, and the potential negative outcome. *Example: Failure of Component X to meet load requirements, causing system outage and resulting in lost user transactions.*

## **2\. Risk Assessment**

*Determine the risk score (Likelihood x Impact) to prioritize.*

| Metric | Rating (1-5) | Justification |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **Likelihood (L)** | \[Select 1-5\] | Detail the factors that make the risk likely or unlikely (e.g., reliance on new tech, lack of expertise, high complexity). |
| **Impact (I)** | \[Select 1-5\] | Detail the consequences if the risk occurs (e.g., cost overrun, schedule delay, reputational damage, financial loss). |
| **Risk Score (L x I)** | \[Calculated Score\] | Define the required response based on this score (Extreme, High, Medium, Low). |
| **Proximity** | \[Immediate / Near-Term / Long-Term\] | When is this risk expected to occur or manifest? |
